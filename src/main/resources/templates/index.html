Software/Web Developer

I'm an Iron Yard Web Development graduate and a french horn player. As a musician I use critical thinking to determine how to produce a beautiful sound on the horn. I'm excited to bring my problem solving skills to programming, and am open to relocation.

In September 2016 I was accepted into a pilot 12-week program by SC Codes which focused on building front-end web applications and also gave me the opportunity to contribute to Code for Greenville's open source project. I enrolled in The Iron Yard's 12-week immersive coding bootcamp in May 2017 where I was able to dive deeper into front-end as well as learning back-end fundamentals.

My interest in working with data and the server side led me to choose Java (Spring Boot) as my language focus. I also have experience with SQL, PostgreSQL, Mongo, Node.js, along with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I enjoy the challenge of debugging and testing, creating databases, and figuring out how to write code that meets the design objectives.

Check out my projects page for samples of my work. I look forward to talking with you about becoming a member of your team!
